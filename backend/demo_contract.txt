EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is made this day by and between the Company and the Employee.

1. Term of Employment
The Employee will be employed on an at-will basis.

2. Confidentiality
Employee agrees not to disclose any trade secrets.

3. Non-Competition
Employee agrees not to work for any competitor for a period of 5 years following the termination of employment, anywhere within the United States. 

4. Limitation of Liability
The Company's liability for any claims arising out of this employment shall be strictly capped at one month's salary, regardless of the cause of action.
